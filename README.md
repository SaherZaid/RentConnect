RentConnect is a peer-to-peer rental platform that enables users to rent items they own or borrow items from other users.  
Instead of buying expensive tools or equipment for one-time use, RentConnect makes sharing easier, more affordable, and more sustainable.

---

## ğŸ¯ Purpose & Concept

The main idea behind RentConnect is to build a system that solves a real need. Many people own items they rarely use, while others need the same items occasionally.

RentConnect connects those users through a platform where they can:

- List items they want to rent out  
- Search for available items nearby  
- Request bookings and manage availability  
- Communicate with the owner  
- Track rental status and confirmations  

This creates a community sharing ecosystem that encourages sustainability and reduces waste, while also giving owners a chance to earn money from unused items.
